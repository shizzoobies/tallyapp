Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

MBS Tally is a calorie and weight tracker offered by Mind Body & Spirit Medicine (MBS Medical), a veteran owned telehealth practice in Florida. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By creating an account and using MBS Tally, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

We only collect what you give us to make the app work:

  • Account details. Your email address and a password. Your password is never stored in plain text. We keep only a salted PBKDF2 hash of it, which cannot be reversed back into your password.
  • Profile and goals. Sex, age, height, current and goal weight, target weekly pace, activity level, your preferred units, and whether to credit exercise back to your daily budget. These let the app compute your calorie target.
  • Food and activity logs. The foods you log (name, calories, macronutrients, meal, and where relevant a restaurant name or product barcode), the meal photos you choose to capture, and the exercise entries you add.
  • Weight history. The weights you log over time, which the app smooths into a trend.
  • Sign in token. A session cookie or token so you stay signed in. We do not use advertising trackers or third party analytics.

How we use your information

  • To calculate your daily calorie target and remaining budget.
  • To show your daily log and a smoothed weight trend over time.
  • To estimate calories and macros from a meal photo when you ask for it.
  • To look up product nutrition when you scan a barcode.
  • To keep your account secure and signed in.

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it for advertising. We only share data with the service providers below, and only as needed to provide the features you use.

Meal photos and AI estimation

When you photograph a meal, the image and any description you add are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to produce a calorie and macro estimate. The estimate is an editable draft that you confirm before it is saved. Photos are stored in your private, per account space and are removed when you delete the entry or your account. Barcode photos are never sent to the AI. Barcodes are read on your device.

Barcode lookups

When you scan or enter a barcode, the numeric code is sent to Open Food Facts, an open product database, to retrieve nutrition information. No photo and no personal information are sent with the lookup.

Where your data is stored

MBS Tally runs on Cloudflare. Your account and logs are stored in a Cloudflare D1 database and your meal photos in Cloudflare R2 storage. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Our service providers are:

  • Cloudflare for hosting, database, and photo storage.
  • Anthropic for meal photo calorie estimates.
  • Open Food Facts for barcode product lookups.

Health information

Your weight, calories, and activity are health related, and we treat them as sensitive. They are used only to provide the tracking features in the app. We do not sell this data or use it for advertising. MBS Tally does not currently connect to Apple Health or Google Health Connect. If we add that ability, we will update this policy first and clearly describe what is read and how it is used. Data read from a health platform will be used only to populate your own logs and will not be shared for advertising.

How long we keep your data, and deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from inside the app, in Profile, under Account. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, food and exercise logs, weight history, sign in sessions, and meal photos. This cannot be undone.

Security

Passwords are hashed with salted PBKDF2 and never stored in plain text. Sessions use an HttpOnly cookie on the web. All traffic is served over HTTPS. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Children's privacy

MBS Tally is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Your choices

  • View and update your profile details at any time in the app.
  • Delete individual food, exercise, and weight entries.
  • Delete your entire account and its data from Profile, under Account.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, you can reach us through ka-performancefl.com.
